Tender Notice

It is very important for everyone to be aware of the cost of power per unit of their place. I reside in Uttar Haryana. I started looking for the per unit charge of electricity in Haryana online when I realised that my previous month’s bill was quite high. I was able to find the latest UHBVN power rate. I think you should also monitor the units and determine whether or not your bill is appropriate.

What is the electricity unit charges in Haryana?

Jagan Reddy has already provided the per unit electricity rate for Gurgaon. Let’s check out the UHBVN 2023 electricity unit rate for domestic supply from the below table:

1. If your total power consumption is up to a maximum of 100 units per month:

Units per Month Energy Charges (Paisa / kWh or kVAh)
0 to 50 200 / kWh 
51 to 100 250 / kWh 

2. If your total power consumption in a month is greater than 100 units but less than 800 units:

Units per Month Energy Charges (Paisa / kWh or kVAh)
0 to 150 275 / kWh 
151 to 250 525 / kWh 
251 to 500 630 / kWh 
501 to 800 710 / kWh
